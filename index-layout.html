<dock-panel>

Attributes

dock (optional)left,right,top,bottom,fillWhat direction to dock the panel. A panel without any dock acts as root container
size (optional)Size in px or % (12%, 23px)Size of the panel in the docking direction.

Usage

A <dock-panel> without dock attribute acts as the root container and doesn't need any attributes.
Place <dock-panel> inside any <dock-panel> to dock the panel in the direction given by the attribute dock
